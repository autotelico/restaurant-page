Il Ragazzino Restaurant

Love yourself some Italian food? Then check out our options!

Indulge in the divine pleasure of our Tiramisu – a heavenly blend of espresso-soaked ladyfingers and velvety mascarpone, crowned with a dusting of cocoa. It's a sweet symphony that'll transport you straight to the heart of Italy.

For pasta enthusiasts, our Spaghetti alla carbonara is a savory delight. Picture al dente pasta coated in a creamy sauce of eggs and pecorino Romano cheese, topped with crispy| pancetta. It's a classic Italian dish that promises a satisfying burst of flavor in every forkful.

Savor the comforting layers of our Lasagna – a culinary masterpiece featuring perfectly cooked pasta, rich Bolognese sauce, and a gooey blanket of melted cheese. It's a warm and hearty embrace of authentic Italian home-cooked goodness.
